Conditional Design Exercise


Rules V.1

Person 1 (black pen) draws straight lines on page
Person 2 (green pen) must draw lines intersecting person 1’s lines
Person 3 (pencil) draws circles on the page
Each have 30 seconds to do each step

Rules V.2

Person 1 (black pen) draws straight lines on page
Person 2 (green) must draw lines perpendicularly intersecting person 1’s lines
Person 3 (pencil) draws circles on the page, cannot intersect with any of the previous lines
Each have 30 seconds to do each step
